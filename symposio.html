"Integration through Innovation."

Symposio, the international level student research conference of SIBM Bengaluru is the flagship event of Research committee. “Symposio” attempts to capture the spirit of Innovation through stimulating discussions to scrutinize application of responsible innovation in the various pillars of management through white paper presentations and seminars. Competitors from across the world present their research work in front of the Judging panel and chief guest. This manifests the importance of Research in management.

The theme for the event is “Integration through innovation”.

If you think, you have the ability to create new knowledge, this is the perfect opportunity for you to showcase your ability to analyze and explain the complex world we live in, through the lens of theoretical tools you have collected. So, bolster your research arsenals and come at SIBM B to compete with the best minds at Revelations’19, an international level student Research Conference.

Sub Themes for Papers:

1. Finance

• Role of environmental, sustainable and governance factors in assessment of stock performance.
• Innovation for financial inclusion.
• Integration of technology in financial markets.
• Innovative practices in risk assessment.

2. Operations

• Anticipatory logistics and supply chain management.
• Influence of big data analytics in effective inventory management
• Big Data and its role in revolutionizing the supply chain for online retailers
• Organizing sustainability competencies through quality management

3. Marketing

• Innovation in digital marketing.
• Integration of marketing and customer satisfaction.
• Integration of CSR in marketing.
• Innovation through integration of social media and traditional media.

4. Human Resource

• Analytics through human resource management.
• Role of human resource in integrating innovation
• Green human resource management for sustainability

This is a 2 Round Event:

The first round is a screening round in which participants are required to submit the abstract of their research work in less than 300 words.
In the second (final) round, shortlisted participants would be required to come down to SIBM Bengaluru campus to present their research work in front of a panel.
Disclaimer:
The topics are only indicative. Participants are encouraged to explore areas beyond the topics listed while keeping the theme in mind.
Participants can also present their paper through Skype.

Round 1 : Abstract Submission

Participants have to submit an abstract on their research topic.
Mail the abstracts to students.research@sibm.edu.in
Research topic should not be a generic one but the ones on which the participants have worked or are currently working.

Round 2 : (Final Round) On-Campus Round

The participants, who have been shortlisted for the final round, would have to present their analysis to a panel of judges on the day of Symposio 2019 in SIBM B campus.
Each participant would be allotted 10 minutes to present & 5 minutes for Q&A sessions.
Decision taken by the judges and the Symposio team will be considered as final and binding..

Guidelines

The abstract should contain a maximum of 300 words..
Mail the abstracts to students.research@sibm.edu.in
Evidence/ proof of Research work has to be submitted on the day of Symposio. Strictly adhere to the deadlines.

Important Dates

1. Abstract submission (300 words) closes on – 29th December,2018
2. Final Paper Submission closes on – 9th January,2019
3. Declaration of Shortlist – 12th January,2019
4. Final Presentation on Campus/Skype- 18th January, 2019.
